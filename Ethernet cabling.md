# Ethernet cabling

Early version of [[Ethernet]] used coax cable. Later evolved to twisted pair and then fibre optics.

Annotation : $10Base5$
Where 10 is the maximum [[Bandwidth]]. Base meaning baseband transmission for [[Ethernet]]. 5 is the type of cabling.
